How to get Started.

01

Create an Account

Visit Katalyst website and click on "Become an Instructor." Fill in your details, including name, email, and a secure password, to register and create your instructor account.

02

Complete Your Profile

Build your instructor profile by adding information about your qualifications, teaching experience, and expertise. A comprehensive profile helps students understand your background and teaching style.

03

Course Creation

Develop engaging content, set objectives, and customize the learning experience. Incorporate multimedia elements to enhance your courses.

04

Submit for Review

After creating your course, submit it for review by Katalyst's team. Once approved, your course will be live on the platform, allowing you to share your knowledge with students worldwide.
