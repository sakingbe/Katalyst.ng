Katalyst For Students

Katalyst is an online educational platform that provides secondary school students with access to learning resources, including courses, videos, quizzes, and assessments, to enhance their learning experience.

Katalyst allows students to sign up and log in to access diverse range of courses and resources prepared by expert instructors and teachers which are designed to be engaging and interactive. Students can enroll, access the materials at their own pace.

Katalyst covers a lot of subjects aligned with the Nigerian and British curriculum. Subjects like arts, science, technology, language and more.

Katalyst is accessible from anywhere in the world, you can gain access to katalyst contents through the web-app and the app (Android and iOS)

Yes, there are a lot of free courses on Katalyst, but there are also different pricing options available depending on the student's needs and budget.

Katalyst tracks student progress through a variety of assessments, quizzes, and assignments, allowing students to see their progress and identify areas for improvement.

To sign up for katalyst, you can visit the website and follow the instructions to create an account.

The courses are delivered through a variety of mediums, including videos, text-based content, live-class sessions, interactive quizzes, and many more.

Katalyst For Educators

Katalyst is an online learning management system designed specifically for instructor, content creators and educators to create, manage and deliver courses online.

To sign up for katalyst, simply go to the website and click on the "Sign Up" button. You will be prompted to provide some basic information about yourself and your organization before you can create an account.

Katalyst offers features such as course creation, management tools, communication tools, assessment, grading tools, multimedia content creation, management, analytics and reporting tools.

Yes, you can create and manage courses on katalyst. You can easily create and organize content, create assessments and assignments, and track student progress.

Katalyst provides various communication tools such as messaging, forums, and announcements to enable instructors to communicate with their students.

You can upload and share various types of content such as videos, images, documents, gamified and interactive quiz and audio files with your students on katalyst.

Katalyst provides various marketing tools such as email campaigns, social media integrations, and referral programs to enable instructors to market their courses and attract students. You can also use your own marketing strategies to promote your courses.

Yes, there is a fee to use katalyst. The pricing depends on the plan you choose and the number of users you have.

Yes, there is customer support available for instructors and educators using katalyst. You can contact customer support via email or phone to get assistance with any questions or technical issues you may have.

Katalyst uses various security measures such as encryption, firewalls, and access controls to protect your content and your students' information.

Our refund policy varies by course. Please review the specific course's refund policy.

To upgrade, visit your account settings and select a premium plan.

Katalyst is designed to be accessible and compatible with screen readers and various accessibility tools.

Please use our contact information to provide feedback or report any issues with the platform. Your input is valuable to us.
